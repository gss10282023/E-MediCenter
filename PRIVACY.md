# Privacy & disclaimer

E‑MediCenter is a **demo/educational** project.

## Do not use real data

Do **not** enter or store real personal/medical information in this application (including names, addresses, symptoms, prescriptions, or any other health-related data).

This project is **not** a medical device and does **not** provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
Any outputs (including AI-generated responses) are for demonstration purposes only.

If you configure optional third‑party APIs (e.g. Google Maps, OpenAI), requests may be sent to those providers according to your configuration and usage.
